French Toast

Description

This is a delicious French toast recipe which incorporates the use of flour for a fluffy end result.

The recipe yields 6 servings, but it can be adjusted to serve more. Feel free to add the toppings of your choice, such as fruits, syrup, or powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 1/2 eggs
  • 1/2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 pinch salt
  • 6 thick slices of bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk.
  2. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled pan over medium heat.
  4. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  5. Cook bread on the preheated pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
